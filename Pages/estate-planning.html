What is estate planning? Estate planning is the strategic coordination of trusts, beneficiary designations, gifting strategies, and wealth transfer structures to ensure assets pass to the right people, in the right way, at the right time — minimizing estate and transfer taxes while preserving family wealth across generations.

Estate & Legacy

Protecting What You Build.
For the People You Love.

Estate planning is about more than documents — it's about making sure the wealth you've built passes to the right people, in the right way, at the right time. We integrate estate strategy into your financial plan so nothing falls through the cracks.

From beneficiary designations to charitable structures, from GRATs and SLATs to dynasty trusts, we look at your estate holistically — how it intersects with your investments, your tax situation, and your family's multigenerational goals.

We work alongside your team — or build one for you.

Estate planning requires close coordination with estate attorneys and CPAs. We work seamlessly alongside your existing advisors, providing the financial context they need to do their best work. If you don't have an estate attorney or tax professional you trust, we can connect you with experienced practitioners in our network who are well-suited to your situation.

How this face connects to the others

Changing a beneficiary designation can affect your estate tax exposure. A trust structure can unlock new gifting strategies. We coordinate these decisions across your entire plan.

What's included

Beneficiary Designation Review
Systematic review of all accounts to ensure designations align with your current wishes and estate plan.
Trust & Will Coordination
Coordinating with your estate attorney on revocable and irrevocable trust structures, asset titling, and pour-over provisions.
Gifting & Advanced Trust Structures
Annual exclusion gifting, 529 superfunding, GRATs, SLATs, IDGTs, and dynasty trusts — coordinated to maximize lifetime exemptions and minimize generation-skipping transfer tax (GSTT) exposure.
Charitable Giving Structures
Donor-advised funds, charitable remainder trusts, qualified charitable distributions, and legacy giving strategies.
Business Succession Planning
Structuring the transfer of business ownership in a way that protects family wealth and minimizes transfer taxes.
Estate & Transfer Tax Exposure Analysis
Modeling estate tax, gift tax, and generation-skipping transfer tax liability under current and projected law — including sunset scenarios for the 2025 exemption levels — to identify multigenerational planning opportunities now.
