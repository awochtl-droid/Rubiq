01 / Investment Management

Your Portfolio, Built
Around Your Life

We build and manage portfolios with discipline and purpose — not around benchmarks, but around you. Every allocation decision flows from a deep understanding of your risk tolerance, time horizon, liquidity needs, and long-term objectives.

We favor evidence-based approaches — diversification, cost-efficiency, and tax awareness — while maintaining the flexibility to act on meaningful opportunities when they arise. And because your portfolio doesn't exist in isolation, every investment decision is stress-tested against your tax picture, retirement timeline, and estate goals.

How this face connects to the others

A portfolio rebalance can trigger capital gains. A large sale can shift your tax bracket and affect your estate plan. We model these interactions before we act.

What's included

Asset Allocation & Portfolio Construction
Custom allocations across equities, fixed income, alternatives, and real assets.
Tax-Aware Rebalancing
Disciplined rebalancing that accounts for realized gains, lot selection, and holding periods.
Manager & Fund Due Diligence
Rigorous evaluation of active managers, passive vehicles, and alternative investment structures.
Alternative Investments
Access to private credit, real estate, and other alternatives appropriate to your profile and liquidity needs.
Concentrated Position Management
Strategies to diversify large single-stock positions while managing the tax impact over time.
Ongoing Reporting & Review
Clear, consolidated performance reporting and regular portfolio reviews aligned to your goals.

Questions we help you answer

How should I think about risk given where I am in life?

When and how should I rebalance without triggering unnecessary taxes?

Does private credit or alternative exposure make sense in my portfolio?

02 / Financial Planning

A Roadmap for
Every Stage of Life

Financial planning is the connective tissue of your entire wealth picture. It translates your goals — buying a home, funding education, navigating a career transition, growing a business — into clear, actionable strategies.

Life rarely moves in a straight line. We revisit your plan continuously, adjusting as your circumstances evolve. When one face of the cube shifts, we reorient the whole picture to keep you on track.

How this face connects to the others

A salary increase changes your savings rate, your tax bracket, and your Roth eligibility — all at once. We map those implications before you make the first move.

What's included

Cash Flow & Savings Analysis

Insurance & Risk Review

Education Funding Strategies
529 plans, Coverdell accounts, UGMA/UTMA, and financial aid positioning for your family's goals.
Major Life Transition Planning
Marriage, divorce, inheritance, business sale, relocation — we model the financial impact of what's coming.
Debt Management & Balance Sheet Optimization
Strategic guidance on mortgages, lines of credit, and the right use of leverage in your financial picture.

Questions we help you answer

How do I financially prepare for a major life change?

03 / Retirement Planning

The Retirement You
Envision, Made Durable

Retirement planning is not a destination — it's a decades-long income management problem. We build structured withdrawal strategies that sustain your lifestyle, minimize taxes over time, and adapt to longevity, healthcare costs, and market volatility.

We address every retirement income lever — Social Security claiming, RMD sequencing, Roth conversions, pension elections — and model them together so each decision reinforces the next.

How this face connects to the others

The year you retire changes everything — your tax exposure, your Medicare premiums, your estate plan. We stress-test your retirement date against every dimension before you commit.

What's included

Retirement Readiness Analysis
Monte Carlo projections and scenario modeling to stress-test your plan against market and longevity risk.
Social Security & Medicare Optimization
Claim timing analysis across spousal strategies to maximize lifetime income and minimize IRMAA surcharges.
IRA / 401(k) / Roth Strategy
Account type optimization, Roth conversion windows, and backdoor strategies for high-income earners.
Tax-Efficient Withdrawal Sequencing
Coordinating distributions from taxable, tax-deferred, and tax-free accounts to minimize lifetime tax drag.
RMD Planning & Management
Proactive required minimum distribution planning to avoid penalties and manage income bunching.
Pension & Annuity Election Guidance
Lump-sum vs. annuity analysis and survivor benefit election guidance tailored to your household situation.

Questions we help you answer

Can I retire when I want to — and will it last as long as I need?

When should I claim Social Security, and how does it interact with my other income?

Should I convert to Roth now, and how much?

04 / Estate & Legacy

Protecting What You Build.
For the People You Love.

Estate planning is about more than documents — it's about making sure the wealth you've built passes to the right people, in the right way, at the right time. We integrate estate strategy into your financial plan so nothing falls through the cracks.

From beneficiary designations to charitable structures, we look at your estate holistically — how it intersects with your investments, your tax situation, and your family's unique dynamics.

We work alongside your team — or build one for you.

Estate planning requires close coordination with estate attorneys and CPAs. We work seamlessly alongside your existing advisors, providing the financial context they need to do their best work. If you don't have an estate attorney or tax professional you trust, we can connect you with experienced practitioners in our network who are well-suited to your situation.

How this face connects to the others

Changing a beneficiary designation can affect your estate tax exposure. A trust structure can unlock new gifting strategies. We coordinate these decisions across your entire plan.

What's included

Beneficiary Designation Review
Systematic review of all accounts to ensure designations align with your current wishes and estate plan.
Trust & Will Coordination
Coordinating with your estate attorney on revocable and irrevocable trust structures, asset titling, and pour-over provisions.
Gifting Programs
Annual exclusion gifting, 529 superfunding, direct tuition payments, and other strategies to reduce taxable estates.
Charitable Giving Structures
Donor-advised funds, charitable remainder trusts, qualified charitable distributions, and legacy giving strategies.
Business Succession Planning
Structuring the transfer of business ownership in a way that protects family wealth and minimizes transfer taxes.
Estate Tax Exposure Analysis
Modeling your estate tax liability under current and projected law to identify planning opportunities now.

Questions we help you answer

Do I have the right estate documents in place — and are they up to date?

How do I transfer wealth to my children in a way that supports them without creating dependency?

Can I give more to charity in a way that also reduces my estate tax?

05 / Tax Planning

Tax Strategy Woven
Into Everything We Do

We do not treat taxes as an afterthought. Tax awareness is embedded in every investment decision, every retirement conversation, and every estate planning discussion we have. We think in after-tax terms, always.

Whether you're an executive navigating equity compensation, a business owner planning a sale, or a real estate investor structuring a 1031 exchange — your tax situation is unique. We provide planning-level guidance tailored to the specific complexity of your income and assets.

We coordinate with your CPA — or bring one to the table.

We provide tax planning and strategy — not tax preparation. For implementation, we work in close coordination with your CPA or tax attorney, ensuring that what we plan is executed properly. If you don't have a trusted tax professional, we can refer you to experienced practitioners who specialize in the complexity of high-net-worth and business-owner tax situations.

How this face connects to the others

Every Roth conversion, every charitable gift, every portfolio rebalance has a tax dimension. We model these interactions across your entire plan — not in isolation.

What's included

Year-Round Proactive Tax Strategy
Ongoing monitoring and planning throughout the year — not just at tax time — to identify and act on opportunities.
Tax-Loss Harvesting
Systematic harvesting of losses to offset gains and defer tax liability without altering your investment exposure.
Equity Compensation Planning
RSU vesting, NQSOs, ISOs, ESPP — navigating the tax treatment, exercise timing, and holding decisions for executives.
Real Estate & 1031 Exchange Strategy
Depreciation planning, 1031 exchange structuring, passive activity analysis, and cost segregation opportunities.
Business Owner Tax Strategy
Entity structure analysis, Section 199A deductions, defined benefit plan contributions, and exit tax planning.
Roth Conversion & Bracket Management
Identifying conversion windows, managing IRMAA thresholds, and filling lower brackets before rates change.

Questions we help you answer

How do I reduce my tax bill this year without sacrificing long-term growth?

What are the tax implications of selling my business or a major investment property?

How do I handle my RSUs without triggering an unexpected tax bill?
