Andreas Wochtl

Founder & Private Wealth Advisor

CFP® MBA · West Chester University 20+ Years Experience
Ultra-High-Net-Worth Families Entrepreneurs Real Estate Investors

Andreas founded Rubiq Financial Partners to deliver truly independent, fiduciary wealth management to entrepreneurs, real estate investors, and high-net-worth families. A CFP® professional with an MBA from West Chester University, he brings over 20 years of financial services experience — including roles at Citizens Securities and Wells Fargo — before establishing Rubiq to serve clients without the conflicts inherent in the wirehouse model. Based in Chester County, PA, he also serves on the board of the Swedish American Chamber of Commerce, advising international business owners across the Philadelphia region.

Areas of Focus

Goals-based wealth planning for ultra-high-net-worth families, entrepreneurs, and real estate investors — modeled across multiple life scenarios, not built around products

Creative tax structuring — minimizing drag across income, capital gains, real estate, and estate transfer through proactive, integrated planning

Liquidity event planning, real estate portfolio optimization, and multi-generational wealth transfer for business owners and investors

Retirement income planning — sequencing Social Security, RMDs, and Roth conversions to maximize after-tax wealth over the full retirement horizon

Real estate investor strategy — managing exits from portfolios, 1031 exchanges, and Qualified Opportunity Zone (QOZ) investments to defer and reduce capital gains
